CHANNEL PARTNER PROGRAM – SALES TOOLBOX & PRM PLATFORM

PROCESS TO PARTNER WITH AVIATRIX

  • 1: Go To Aviatrix Partners Website (www.AviatrixPartners.com) - Request And Download Aviatrix Reseller Agreement & NDA, Review the Channel Partner Program Overview
  • 2: Return Executed NDA & Reseller Agreement as an Attachment on the Aviatrix Partner Site
  • 3: Return Executed N Aviatrix Sales and Marketing Firm Captjur Will Review Your Submission and Be In Contact With Additional Questions or Requests
  • 4: Captjur Will Discuss Program Requirements and Entry Levels for the Program & Discuss The On-Boarding Process
  • 5: Once the Above Is Complete You Will then be referred for setup with Aviatrix Directly or Through a Marketplace Partner
  • 6: You Will Be Contacted By The Aviatrix and Captjur Team for On-Boarding and Initial Trainings Schedule
  • 7: Partner is provided username and access code to the partner online sales toolbox and setup in our PRM Platform (Partner Relationship Management)
  • 8: You are an Official Partner and You Will Have Access to All Program Elements at This Point.

PARTNER LEVEL REQUIREMENTS

GOLD – (ATPL)

Highest Level Partner

  • 10+ Instances Deployed Per Year
  • Full Hands-On Demonstrations & Support
  • Discounts on Larger Deals
  • High Team Support of Business (Captjur)
  • Multiple Staff Trained and Certified with Aviatrix within (2) Months
  • Expertise & Knowledge Base in Cloud Network Security Market

SILVER – (CPL)

Mid Level Partner

  • 7+ Instances Deployed Per Year
  • Limited Hands-On Demonstrations & Support
  • Discounts on Larger Deals
  • Medium Team Support of Business (Captjur)
  • At Least One Staff Member Trained and Certified with Aviatrix ACE within (2) Months
  • Beginning Knowledge Base in Cloud Network Security Market

Bronze – (PPL)

Entry Level Partner

  • 4+ Instances Deployed Per Year
  • On-Line Only Demonstrations & Support
  • No Discounts on Larger Deals, Special Pricing Agreements Only
  • Low Team to Support of Business (Online – PRM Platform)
  • One Staff Member Trained and Certified with Aviatrix ACE within one year
  • Willing to Gain Expertise & Knowledge Base in Cloud Network Security Market
